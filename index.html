seeing old things in new ways



mission

To foster closer relationships and softer ways of knowing (eg. knowing a person vs. knowing a fact) the natural world through immersive experiences where people spend quality time with each other

  • observing the natural world
  • documenting what they sense and experience
  • make meaning of their observations with diverse tools and forms of storytelling.

In the process, they'll cultivate new ways knowing and new patterns of relating to themselves, each other, and "the natural world."

who

People interested in spending time outside observing and making things.
