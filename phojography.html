phojography + extensions

In the spring, I made a friend by walking and talking together. On walks, I pointed out things that I saw in the world (eg. seed pods, fruits, flowers, the sky). Like someone with an odd sense of humor coming across a friend who appreciates their jokes, my enthusiasm for the natural world had a new place to express itself...in this friendship. On my daily jog, I started taking and texting photographs of plants we had seen together.
