phojography + extensions

On my daily jog, I started taking and texting photographs of plants and animals to a friend. As I photographed more and shared more, I saw more patterns, textures, specific species and how they developed over time...

Each photograph below represents something I began to see as a result of the habits I cultivated while taking photographs. Click on each image to see the "pattern" it represents.

This project isn't finished. I hope it'll never be and will continue to accrete as I get better at seeing old things in new ways.;)
